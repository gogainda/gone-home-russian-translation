The United States of America

Certificate of Naturalization

On July 20, 1972 the Court having found that Janice Elizabeth Connelly Greenbriar, then residing at 1010 Fifth Avenue, Seattle, Wash., intends to reside permanently in the United States and had complied with the applicable provisions of such naturalization laws, and was entitled to be admitted to citizenship thereupon ordered that such person be, and (s)he was admitted as a citizen of the United States of America.

In testimony whereof the seal of the Court is hereunder affixed this 20th day of July in the year of our Lord nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

Horace E. Colver
Clerk of the U.S. District Court
