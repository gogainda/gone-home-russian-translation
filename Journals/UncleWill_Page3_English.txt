3. I do hereby bequeath every item of value of which I may die possessed, including the dwelling and surrounding acres located at 1 Arbor Hill, as well as any and all personal property and monied accounts, to Terrence L. Greenbriar, Jr., of Ellis County.

I subscribe my name to this Will this 13 day of August, 1973, at the offices of the Boon County Court Registrar.

Oscar Masan
